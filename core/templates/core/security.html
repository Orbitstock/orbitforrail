Security

How safe is Orbit Stock Index to use?

Your funds are yours and only yours!

Orbit Stock Index does not have access to your funds and nor would we want to – they are kept securely at the data center and connected by fully encrypted API keys.

API enables our platform to execute and generate an asset portfolio in a secure way – API does not reveal any information. Your password and all personal information is held securely.

Safer than your internet bank

All information delivered to our servers is encrypted with 2048-bit standard protocols and stored on a secure network, protected by firewall. No one has access to your data and the high security standards makes Orbit Stock Index twice as safe as most internet banks!

Extra layers of security.

We strongly recommend using 2FA not only for Orbit Stock Index but also for the email address associated with your account. This will add one more security and safety measure to make sure your account is kept safe.
